"Sugar, spice, and a large pizza slice." About Our Shop

Founded in 2020 by the Lucky sisters, our establishment has been serving up rich pizza sourced from artisan farmers in various regions of East and West Malaysia. We are dedicated to travelling the world, finding the best ingredients, and bringing back to you here in our shop.

We guarantee that you will fall in lust with our decadent ingredients the moment you walk inside until you finish your last slice. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
