Frequent Asked Questions

1. Question: What should I do when I forgot my password?

Answer: Click "Forgot password?" button at Login page and recover your account by providing the required information.

2. Question: Do I have to pay for the membership?

Answer: It’s all very simple. You earn One (1) Slice for every RM30 spent, with any qualifying online order (delivery and self - collection, only). When you’ve earned enough Slices for a reward, you can choose to redeem them in exchange for the reward listed in the PizzaCiousss Rewards Menu.

3. Question: How do I redeem the rewards?

Answer: To enjoy the rewards, choose the items you prefer and add into the basket top confirm on the redemption of the reward. Once confirmed, the slices will be deducted based on the rewards you have choose.

For Example, total 12 slices available and I choose to redeem the Pasta at the slices required 9 Slices. Once confirmed, a total of 9 Slices will be deducted in your account and remaining balance is at 3 slices.

4. Question: Will my slice expired?

Answer: Yes! In three (3) months, starting from the date that you earned your slices. This is a rolling time frame, which means that if you earn them in September 30th 2019, then it will expire on December 30th 2019.

5. Question: What do I do when I didn’t receive a slice after 24 hours?

Answer: Get in touch with our PizzaCiousss Customer Service at (317) 585-8468
